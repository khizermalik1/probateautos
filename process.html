How Our Probate Vehicle Process Works

  1. Step 1 — Get in Touch

    You contact us by phone or through our valuation form. We take a few basic details about the vehicle and your situation.

  2. Step 2 — Receive Your Personal Valuation

    A specialist reviews the information and provides an initial valuation based on the vehicle’s age, condition and market value.

  3. Step 3 — We Arrange an Inspection

    If you’re happy to continue, we book a convenient time for one of our inspectors to visit.

  4. Step 4 — On‑Site Assessment

    Our inspector checks the vehicle in person — classic, non‑runner or barn find — confirming the details you provided.

  5. Step 5 — Payment on the Day

    Once the inspection is complete and everything matches, we arrange payment there and then.

  6. Step 6 — We Handle the Paperwork

    We take care of DVLA paperwork, probate documentation and any issues with missing V5s or SORN vehicles.

  7. Step 7 — Our Transport Team Collects the Vehicle

    Our in‑house transport team collects the vehicle safely from your home, garage or storage location.
