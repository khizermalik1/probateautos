Our Story

Hadleys Probate Autos began as a simple idea: make the difficult task of dealing with a deceased relative's vehicle straightforward and stress-free. What started as a local service helping neighbours and friends has grown into a trusted nationwide team. We combine practical experience with clear communication so executors and families get a fair valuation, a reliable inspection and a secure collection.

We know this is often a sensitive time. Our approach is practical and compassionate: we explain every step, provide written offers, and arrange payment and collection at a time that suits the family. No jargon, no pressure, just a straightforward service from people who care.

Our Values

  • Respectful service — we treat every family and vehicle with care and discretion
  • Transparent valuations — clear, written offers with no hidden fees
  • Secure payment — funds arranged and confirmed before collection
  • Nationwide collection — insured transport and professional handling across the UK
  • Practical expertise — decades of combined experience valuing classic and modern vehicles
